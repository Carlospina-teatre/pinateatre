English Stories

The Three Little Pigs

Charlie has arrived from New York to tell The Three Little Pigs. This classic story is perfect for young and older audiences, who can easily follow the narrative while practicing vocabulary such as numbers, colors, emotions, and materials.

We also explore musical styles like rock, rap, and electronic music, as each pig represents a different musical style. This encourages the audience to sing, dance, and enjoy the performance actively.

The story uses objects, puppets, and shadows to support storytelling and motivate audience participation in English.

Pied Piper of Hamelin

Charlie has arrived from New York to tell Pied Piper of Hamelin, a classic story that helps introduce music and wind instruments like accordion, flute, melodica, and more.

The tale emphasizes the importance of unity, obedience, and respect among citizens while practicing English vocabulary related to animals and their characteristics.

#PiedPiper #EnglishStorytelling #MusicEducation #Values #PinaTeatre

Pinocchio

Charlie has arrived from New York to tell Pinocchio. The classic story transforms into a participatory tale where the audience accompanies the little puppet on his journey towards responsibility, truth, and friendship.

Objects and symbolic elements represent the story actively, stimulating imagination and audience reconstruction of the narrative.

Themes include honesty, learning from mistakes, making good decisions, and caring for others, with English vocabulary practice related to emotions, actions, objects, and body parts.

The show combines humor, rhythm, live music, and participatory games, inviting everyone to sing, move, and enjoy a dynamic theatrical experience.

#Pinocchio #EnglishStorytelling #Values #Learning #Theatre #PinaTeatre

Little Red Riding Hood

Charlie has arrived from New York to tell Little Red Riding Hood. The story uses costumes and objects to bring the tale to life, fostering imagination and creativity in the audience.

Music, including guitar, supports the storytelling while teaching English vocabulary and encouraging participation.
