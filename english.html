The Three Little Pigs

Charlie has arrived from New York to tell The Three Little Pigs. This classic story is perfect for young and older audiences, practicing vocabulary such as numbers, colors, emotions, and materials.

We also explore musical styles like rock, rap, and electronic music. Each pig represents a different musical genre, encouraging the audience to sing, dance, and participate.

The show includes objects, puppets, and shadows to enrich storytelling and motivate English participation.

Pied Piper of Hamelin

Charlie has arrived from New York to tell Pied Piper of Hamelin, introducing music and wind instruments like accordion, flute, and melodica.

The story highlights unity, obedience, and respect while practicing vocabulary about animals and characteristics.

#PiedPiper #EnglishStorytelling #MusicEducation #Values #PinaTeatre

Pinocchio

Charlie has arrived from New York to tell Pinocchio, transforming the classic tale into a participatory experience focused on responsibility, truth, and friendship.

Symbolic objects help reconstruct the story, stimulating imagination and interaction.

Vocabulary includes emotions, actions, objects, and body parts. The show mixes humor, live music, and dynamic games.

#Pinocchio #EnglishStorytelling #Values #Learning #Theatre #PinaTeatre

Little Red Riding Hood

Charlie tells Little Red Riding Hood using costumes and objects that bring the story to life, fostering creativity.

Music, including guitar, supports storytelling and encourages English participation.
